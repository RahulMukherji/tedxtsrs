TEDxTSRS 2017

The Shri Ram Schools, founded in 1988 by Mrs. Manju Bharat Ram, follow her personal principle of promoting individuality through value-based education; this promotes a global outlook, yet retains intrinsic Indian cultural traditions.

Here, we believe that education is not only a tool to achieve professional milestones, but also a means to make lives better. Every student is considered to be unique and we strive to offer a platform that lets them discover their own special light, at their own pace.

Each student is provided a diverse education in a safe, supportive environment that promotes sound values, self discipline, motivation, and excellence in learning. The students are assisted in developing skills to become independent self-sufficient adults, to succeed, and contribute responsibly in a global community.

The motto, "Vidya Dadaati Vinayam" - from knowledge comes humility, perfectly encapsulates the progressive, secular, and child-centered nature that these co-educational private schools possess; The Shri Ram Schools are committed to providing quality education for all the students. The philosophy rests on the interpretation of 'education' as drawing forth what already exists. Education imparted results in well-formed minds and the impetus and environment to think.

TED is a nonprofit organisation devoted to spreading ideas in the form of short, powerful talks. It began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers a variety of topics from science to business, and global issues. In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx, an independently organized TED event, is a program of local, self-organized events that brings people together to share a TED-like experience; one in which the speakers spark deep discussions and connections in small groups. These local, self-organized Youth events are one of our most creative TEDx event types, organized by, and catered towards kids.
